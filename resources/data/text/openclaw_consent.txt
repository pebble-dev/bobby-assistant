Clawd connects to your OpenClaw bot via Telegram to process requests.

Important:
- All your messages and requests are sent through Telegram to your OpenClaw instance
- This app does not control the OpenClaw server - it is self-hosted by you or a third party
- Data handling, retention, and privacy depend on your OpenClaw configuration
- Your Telegram account and OpenClaw bot username are stored locally on this device

By continuing to use Clawd, you agree to these terms.
